The Godot Game Engine

Published 3 days ago


Sharing my experience with the open-source Godot Engine and showcasing my small game.

In search of a free game engine, I found out about Godot, an open-source game engine!

Why Godot?

Learning GDScript, godot's native programming language, wasn't extremely difficult. Its simple syntax is similar to Python, a language that I was already familiar with!

I chose that specific engine over Unity because of its tiny size and smooth performance!

Thoughts On Game Development

Creating games is definitely fun, but I personally had a lot of trouble developing something that actually looked good. I'm a horrible artist and drawing pixelart seemed to be my only choice.

Even though writing code was exciting, art and sound effects were a pain in the neck. Well, here's my itch.io page. To be honest, I am not particularly proud of that game, but I sure may create another game in the near future.
